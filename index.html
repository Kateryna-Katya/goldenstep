About the Game

From simple merges to a living world

It's like any other merge game you've probably played, except this time you're not just matching stuff for points - you're actually creating something cool. You match up the same items to get new stuff, and all of that helps you build up your little cat town. What's really cool is that every single merge you do actually changes your town somehow, and that instant feedback is weirdly addictive.
